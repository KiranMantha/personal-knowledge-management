## What is it?

This strategy consists of a Double Supertrend, which implies that we’re using an indicator called a Supertrend, but we’re using it twice, with tweaked settings for each one that is.

This is the specific indicator you want to be looking for.

Press enter or click to view image in full size

![]()

We’ll leave the settings for the first of our two Supertrends completely default and on the second one, we’re going to change the AR multiplier from 3 to 2, leaving all the rest default.

The second piece of this strategy consists of the On-Balance Volume Indicator. If you haven’t ever heard of it or have no clue on how to use it, don’t worry. I just wrote an article on how to use it last week:

<https://medium.com/@BitcoinShrimps/a-trading-turtorial-on-on-balance-volume-obv-c9db4f8e8747>

We’ll be using the OBV to help determine when a trend could soon break and thus when we should consider closing our position.

## How to use it

### 1. Draw your OBV trendlines

First off, we’ll be scouting for the current trendlines that we can find on the OBV.

In my example, this is a pretty clear example of a downtrend. We’ve been in this one ever since we lost 40K all the way back in May. I recommend you putting an alert on this trendline so that you will immediately be made aware when the trendline is (or touched again and another leg down).

### 2. When the trendline breaks & both Supertrends are in the same direction, trade the opposite of the current Supertrend.

When an uptrend is broken, short.

When a downtrend is broken, long.

### 3. Hold untill Supertrend with ATR Multiplier = 3 flips to the opposite trend

Now wait and sit back.

Set your take profit at the least active of the Supertrends (the one where we left the settings default) and your stop loss at the recent swing High/Low.

If the OBV trend breaks, that’s also a sign to exit your position.
