If you’re like me, you’ve probably come across countless SaaS idea videos that make you roll your eyes. They often throw around ideas with zero real-world validation.

But don’t worry! Today, we’re diving into 10 micro SaaS ideas that are not just “good on paper” but are actually generating real revenue.

Yes, you heard that right! These ideas are backed by data from Reddit, so you know they’ve been tried, tested, and are successfully bringing in money.

Let’s get into it!

## 1. Scrabio — $500 MRR in 2 Months

First up, we have [Scrabio](https://scrabio.com/). This tool scrapes data from businesses running Google ads, making it perfect for marketing agencies hunting for clients of specific sizes. For just $50 a month, you get access to contact details for 5,000 businesses and 7-day support.

Scrabo claims it can significantly uplift your response and booking rates, plus they offer a 7-day money-back guarantee (just remember to read the fine print!).

While it might only be available for U.S. contacts, achieving $500 in monthly recurring revenue (MRR) in two months is quite an achievement!

## 2. Famewall — $1,000 MRR in 12 Months

If you’re a website owner, [Famewall](https://famewall.io/) could be a game-changer! This micro SaaS helps you effortlessly collect and display testimonials. With a customizable link for gathering reviews and multiple embedding options (think awards and badges), it has built a loyal customer base, all while clocking in at $1,000 MRR. Talk about a must-have for boosting credibility!

## 3. ClickPilot — $1,600 MRR in 5 Months

Creators, this one’s for you! [ClickPilot](https://clickpilot.app/) lets users compare YouTube video thumbnails. It’s all about making sure your visuals stand out from the crowd. With 7,200 free users and 250 paid subscribers, this app has found its niche. At just $10 a month, it’s a widely accessible solution for content creators looking to enhance their engagement.

## 4. GrowthPanels — $2,000 MRR in 2 Months

Jumping up, we find [GrowthPanels](https://growthpanels.com/), which helps founders reward customer actions with discounts. Designed specifically for SaaS companies, it incentivizes customer engagement right within subscription platforms. The blend of a free tier and seamless integration has worked wonders for their early success, hitting $2,000 in MRR in just two months!

## 5. SketchLogo AI — $31,000 MRR in 4 Months

Now, how about something a bit more creative? [SketchLogo AI](https://www.sketchlogo.ai/) combines user sketches with AI to whip up unique logos. If you’re in need of eye-catching designs without breaking the bank, this service offers logos starting at $19/month. In just four months, it’s already pulled in an impressive $31,000 MRR!

## 6. RepurposePie — $5,000 MRR in 3 Days

[RepurposePie](https://repurposepie.io/) converted tweets into videos for TikTok and YouTube, and it’s no surprise it raked in $5,000 MRR within just three days. This type of automation taps into the growing demand for easily sharable content, indicating significant market potential. Quick content transformation? Yes, please!

## 7. FTTSignUp — $5,000 MRR in 2 Months

Next, we have [FTTSignUp](https://fttsignup.com/). This micro SaaS connects legal professionals with clients looking for assistance. By handling marketing for those who might not be as skilled at client acquisition, it generates valuable leads and earns $5,000 MRR within its first couple of months. A win-win for legal pros!

## 8. KeepTheScore — $10,000 MRR

What about live events? Enter [KeepTheScore](https://keepthescore.com/), which helps users create real-time leaderboards and scores for everything from presentations to streaming sessions. With stellar user guides and affordable plans, it’s easy to see how it’s captured the attention of content creators, hitting $10,000 MRR.

## 9. UniconPlatform — $16,000 MRR

[UniconPlatform](https://unicornplatform.com/) is an AI-driven website builder that simplifies website creation for startups and solo entrepreneurs. With a free tier and scalable paid options, it’s a more straightforward alternative to complex platforms like Webflow and WordPress. Recently fetching $800,000 in an acquisition, it showcases how micro SaaS ventures can evolve into serious players in the market.

## 10. ExcelFormulaBot — $40,000 MRR in a Year

Finally, let’s look at [ExcelFormulaBot](http://excelformulabot.com/?rel=hackernoon). This genius uses AI to generate spreadsheet formulas and code. It started as a micro SaaS and has expanded significantly, now generating an impressive $40,000 MRR within a year. Even if it strayed from the micro SaaS category, its initial success is undeniable!

## Wrapping Up

So there you have it! 10 micro SaaS ideas that are not just theoretical but thriving in the real world. If you’re thinking of launching your own SaaS, emulating these successful products is a fantastic starting point. But remember, success isn’t just about the idea — it’s also about how well you market it and monetize it.

Don’t forget that validating your ideas is crucial. You could create landing pages to gauge interest before you dive headfirst into development. Put these insights to work, tap into existing demands, or find a unique twist on these concepts. The SaaS world is bustling with opportunities, so get out there and make your mark!
